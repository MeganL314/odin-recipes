Salmon

Description

Delicious salmon. Can be made with a variety of different spices or just salt and pepper.

Ingredients

  • salmon fillets
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • salt and pepper
  • lemon

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. Season the salmon with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear the salmon until the underside is no longer translucent. This should take about 6-7 minutes depending on the thickness of the salmon.
  3. Add garlic to the skillet.
  4. Flip the salmon and cook another 6-7 minutes.
