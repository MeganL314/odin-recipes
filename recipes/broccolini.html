Broccolini

Description

Delicious broccolini. A great side dish. It is easy to make and tastes great sauted with a few spices.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • broccolini
  • garlic
  • lemon
  • red pepper flakes
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large lidded skiller. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes.
  2. After a few minutes, add the broccolini and salt.
  3. Cook until tender and then toss with lemon juice.
