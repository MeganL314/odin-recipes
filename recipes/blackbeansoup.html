Black Bean Soup

Description

Delicious black bean soup. This recipe can be made with all vegan ingredients by substituting for a vegetable broth. Either canned or dried beans can be used. For a creamier texture, blend or emulsify a portion of the beans.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • onion
  • carrots
  • celery
  • garlic
  • black beans
  • chicken broth
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • chili powder
  • salt, pepper, cayenne, other spices

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pot. Add onion until translucent then garlic, carrots, celery.
  2. Add spices and broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Turn down the heat and let cook for 20 minutes.
  4. Add black beans and cook an additional 10 minutes.
